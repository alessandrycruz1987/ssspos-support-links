Privacy Policy Privacy & Data Sovereignty Last updated: May 2026

Welcome to SSSPOS. We believe that your business data belongs to you. Our architecture is designed to provide absolute autonomy without the need for internet contracts or expensive data plans.

1. Local Ecosystem (Offline First)

SSSPOS transforms your router into a private network. Because we operate 100% offline, your sales information, product lists, and customer interactions never leave your establishment. There is no cloud synchronization, meaning no trackers and no external risks.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect, sell, or trade your business data. All intelligence—including sales charts (daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly) and business insights—is processed and stored locally on your Server device.

3. Server & Client Synchronization

Communication between the Server Mode and Client Mode occurs strictly within your Local WiFi. SSSPOS uses this connection only to sync menus, process orders, and track preparation status in real-time. This data is volatile and remains within your secure local environment.

4. Transparency in the Free Version

The Free version allows up to 15 orders per day in Server mode. This limitation is managed locally by the application logic and does not involve monitoring your business activities from our end.

5. Your Control

By using SSSPOS, you maintain total control. If you choose to uninstall the application or clear its data, all your business information is permanently removed from the device, as we do not keep backups on external servers.

6. Contact

For any questions regarding your privacy or technical setup, please contact our support team through the official channels listed on our home page.
